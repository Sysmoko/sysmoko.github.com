About Sysmoko

We create web and mobile solutions primarily in the education and lifestyle space. Our expertise and passion in the mobile space drives us to creating interesting and exciting products in the marketplace.

We are looking forward to offering our expertise to help your business. If you have any ideas which you will like to share and need help in, please do contact us for a discussion! In the meantime, please check out all our apps available on Apple App Store and Google Play.

Our Process

Sysmoko process is all about innovation, design and development. We work on ideas within the team, and with our clients, by bringing these elements together to create our products. Sysmoko works iteratively based on feedback to ensure that the ideas are brought to life the way we or our clients want it.

Our Approach

Sysmoko approach in developing web mobile apps is about understanding and engaging the problem based on feedback within the team and our client. Sysmoko emphasizes on rapid development and iterating through based on feedback from the team, client or customers. We end the approach by conducting QA iteratively to create the ideal product for you and the marketplace.

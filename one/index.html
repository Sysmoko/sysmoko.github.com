Strategy

Sysmoko understands that journey from idea to product is not that straight forward. We have crafted a process - the Sysmoko process - to help you in this journey, bring the elements of innovation, design and development together to create the product from your idea.

Innovation

Every business in the world today requires innovation to keep abreast of competition in the market place. Sysmoko is here to help you keep yourself ahead of competition with our mobile innovation solutions. Many brilliant and innovative ideas are available out there, and Sysmoko wants to make these ideas a reality.

Design

Design is the first step in translating innovation to reality. Sysmoko is here to work with you to get the look and feel right for the idea. With a blue print, Sysmoko brings you one step closer towards realizing your idea.

About us.

We create apps for education and lifestyle on the mobile platform. And strive to make it fun for all ages. Please do check out the apps we have created so far.

We are looking forward to creating more new apps. If you have any ideas in the mobile space which you will like to share and need help in, please do contact us for a discussion! In the meantime, please do check out all our apps available on Apple App Store and Google Play.

Our Process

Sysmoko process is all about innovation, design and development. We work on ideas within the team, and with our clients, by bringing these elements together to create our products. Sysmoko works iteratively based on feedback to ensure that the ideas are brought to life the way we or our clients want it.

Our Approach

Sysmoko approach in developing mobile apps is about utilizing tools that fit our and our clients' needs, and providing a mutual feedback platform within the team, and with our clients. Some of the tools we use in our approach includes RED, Trello, TestFlight and GitHub, on top of emails, whatsapp/SMS, Phone calls, Google Hangouts we have within the team and our clients. The Sysmoko approach does not end with these mentioned tools, as we actively seek for ways to improve our process and approach in mobile development.
